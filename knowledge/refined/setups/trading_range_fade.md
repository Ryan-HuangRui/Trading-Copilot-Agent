# Setup: Trading Range Fade

## Scope (MANDATORY)
- Market: US Stocks
- Timeframe: Not explicitly specified
- Direction: Reversal
- Style: Mean Reversion

## Market Preconditions
- Market is in a clear trading range.
- Upper and lower boundaries are well defined.

## Entry Triggers
### Short
- Price reaches upper boundary of trading range.
- Reversal signal bar forms.
- OR second-leg trap appears near range high.

### Long
- Price reaches lower boundary of trading range.
- Reversal signal bar forms.

### Entry Method
- Use Stop Orders only.
- Limit orders are considered advanced and not recommended for beginners.

## Invalidation / Failure Conditions
- Strong breakout beyond range boundary.
- Follow-through confirms breakout validity.

## Risk Management
- Stop loss placed outside trading range boundary.

## Profit Taking
- Opposite side of trading range.
- OR mid-range target.

## Common Mistakes
- Chasing price in the middle of the trading range.
