# Setup: 90-Minute Opening Range Breakout

## Scope (MANDATORY)
- Market: US Stocks
- Timeframe: Intraday (5-minute bars)
- Direction: Long OR Short
- Style: Opening Range Breakout

## Market Preconditions
- Only applicable during early intraday session.

## Entry Triggers
- Observe first 18 × 5-minute bars (90 minutes).
- Define high and low of opening range.
- Strong breakout:
  - Close occurs outside range boundary.
- Entry:
  - Trade in direction of breakout.

## Invalidation / Failure Conditions
- Price quickly reverses back into opening range after breakout.

## Risk Management
- Stop loss options:
  - Opposite end of breakout bar.
  - OR midpoint of opening range.

## Profit Taking
- Measured move:
  - Opening range height projected in breakout direction.
