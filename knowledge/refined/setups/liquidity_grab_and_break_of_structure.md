# Setup: Liquidity Grab + Break of Structure (SMC)

## Scope (MANDATORY)
- Market: US Stocks
- Timeframe: Multi-timeframe (HTF + LTF)
- Direction: Reversal → Trend Continuation
- Style: Smart Money Concepts (SMC)

## Market Preconditions
- Price reaches:
  - Higher-timeframe key support/resistance.
  - OR liquidity pool (prior high/low).
- Context aligns with HTF structure.

## Entry Triggers
1. Liquidity Sweep:
   - Price briefly breaks prior high/low.
   - Immediate rejection (long wick / pin).
2. Break of Structure (BOS / MSS):
   - Strong move in opposite direction.
   - Breaks short-term market structure.
3. Return:
   - Price pulls back into:
     - Order Block, OR
     - Fair Value Gap (FVG).
4. Entry:
   - Confirmation signal at return zone.

## Invalidation / Failure Conditions
- Price fully violates Order Block or FVG zone.

## Risk Management
- Stop loss placed beyond extreme of liquidity sweep candle.

## Profit Taking
- Next liquidity pool:
  - Prior high or low.
