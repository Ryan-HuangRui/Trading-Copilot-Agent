# Setup: Strong Breakout (Trend-Following)

## Scope (MANDATORY)
- Market: US Stocks
- Timeframe: Not explicitly specified
- Direction: Long OR Short (trend-following)
- Style: Breakout / Momentum

## Market Preconditions
- Market is in Breakout (Spike) phase OR Tight Channel.
- Presence of strong momentum candles.
- Overlap between candles is minimal.
- Gaps may be present.

## Entry Triggers
- A strong trend bar appears:
  - Large body
  - Small or no wick
- Follow-through:
  - A second strong trend bar in the same direction immediately after the breakout bar.
- Optional gap between bars (no overlap).
- Entry methods:
  - Aggressive: Enter at close of breakout bar.
  - Conservative: Place stop order 1 tick beyond breakout bar high (long) or low (short).

## Invalidation / Failure Conditions
- Breakout bar is followed by:
  - Strong reversal bar, OR
  - Doji indicating lack of follow-through.
- Price immediately returns into the pre-breakout trading range.

## Risk Management
- Stop loss placed beyond the origin of breakout move:
  - Below breakout base for long
  - Above breakout base for short
- Position size must be reduced due to wide stop.

## Profit Taking
- Measured move:
  - Based on breakout bar body
  - Or prior range height
- Typical characteristics:
  - Win rate ≈ 60%
  - Risk–reward ≈ 1:1

## Common Mistakes
- Misidentifying consecutive large bars inside a trading range as a true breakout.
