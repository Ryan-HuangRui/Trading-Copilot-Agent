# Setup: Trend Pullback (High-2 / Low-2)

## Scope (MANDATORY)
- Market: US Stocks
- Timeframe: Not explicitly specified
- Direction:
  - Long in bull trend (High-2)
  - Short in bear trend (Low-2)
- Style: Trend Continuation (Pullback)

## Market Preconditions
- Market is in a trend:
  - Tight Channel OR Broad Channel
- A pullback against the dominant trend is present.

## Entry Triggers
### Long (High-2)
- In an uptrend:
  - Second attempt to resume trend after pullback.
  - Signal bar forms a higher high.
  - Place Buy Stop 1 tick above signal bar high.

### Short (Low-2)
- In a downtrend:
  - Second attempt to resume trend after rally.
  - Signal bar forms a lower low.
  - Place Sell Stop 1 tick below signal bar low.

### Additional Rule
- Pullback often reaches EMA20.
- Reversal signal bar forms near EMA20.

## Invalidation / Failure Conditions
- Price reverses immediately after entry and breaks opposite side of signal bar.
- Pullback extends into:
  - High-3 / High-4
  - OR lasts more than ~20 bars → potential transition to trading range.

## Risk Management
- Stop loss options:
  - Beyond signal bar extreme.
  - Beyond prior major swing high/low.

## Profit Taking
- Prior trend extreme (previous high or low).
- Measured move based on prior swing.
